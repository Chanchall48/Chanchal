Hi, I'm Chanchal

I am a textile and graphic designer passionate about creating home textile that blends art with everyday living. My work is inspired by floral elements, minimal aesthetics, and soft pastel hues, often infused with an embroidered effect. With a keen eye for color harmony and composition, I design pieces that bring warmth, calmness, and elegance to interiors.

I believe home décor should be more than just decoration—it should tell a story and create a comforting atmosphere. Through thoughtful details and creative exploration, I craft unique textile and digital designs that transform spaces into serene and expressive environments.
